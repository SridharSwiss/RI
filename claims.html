The Integrated Analytics Stack Advantage

Our existing technology creates a powerful synergy. Each component plays a distinct, complementary role in realizing our vision of an intelligence-driven claims function.

The Data Intelligence Platform

Databricks is the core engine for our entire data ecosystem. It's where raw, unstructured data from thousands of sources is transformed into high-quality, reliable intelligence. We will leverage it to build and operationalize a suite of predictive models that will fundamentally change how we manage claims, moving from reactive processing to proactive intervention.

  • Medallion Architecture: Implementing a rigorous Bronze-Silver-Gold data pipeline to ensure data quality, reliability, and governance from ingestion to consumption.
  • Predictive Modeling: Deploying ML models for large loss prediction, litigation propensity, and subrogation opportunity scoring.
  • Generative AI: Utilizing LLMs for automated summarization of complex claim notes and legal documents to accelerate adjuster workflows.

The Business Intelligence Layer

Power BI is our window into the claims operation, translating complex data from Databricks into clear, actionable insights for every level of the business. It will democratize data, replacing static spreadsheets with dynamic, interactive dashboards that empower users to ask and answer their own questions, fostering a true data-driven culture.

  • Executive Dashboards: High-level views of portfolio performance, loss ratios, and key financial metrics against targets.
  • Operational Reports: Real-time tracking of adjuster caseloads, cycle times, and bottlenecks to optimize resource allocation.
  • Self-Service Analytics: Empowering business analysts to explore curated datasets and create their own insights without IT dependency.

The Advanced Investigation Workbench

Palantir is our specialized tool for the most complex investigative challenges. It excels where traditional BI tools fall short, allowing investigators to fuse disparate internal and external data sources into a single, unified view. It's indispensable for uncovering sophisticated fraud networks and managing large-scale, chaotic catastrophe events.

  • Fraud Ring Detection: Visualizing hidden connections between claimants, providers, and attorneys to uncover organized fraud schemes that would otherwise go unnoticed.
  • Catastrophe Event Response: Fusing geospatial data, weather alerts, and policy information with incoming claims to manage large-scale events and allocate resources effectively.
  • Complex Claims Investigation: Providing a unified workbench for investigators to analyze unstructured documents, timelines, and entity relationships in high-stakes litigation cases.

100-Day Action Plan

A phased approach to build momentum, deliver early value, and establish data-driven leadership within the first three months.

Phase I (Days 1-30): Discovery & Alignment

The foundational phase is dedicated to deep listening and learning. We will map the human, process, and technical landscape to build a strategy grounded in reality, not assumptions. This ensures we solve the right problems and build trust from day one.

  • Stakeholder Mapping: Conduct structured interviews with claims leadership, adjusters, and IT partners to understand strategic priorities, daily pain points, and perceived roadblocks.
  • Process Immersion: "A day in the life" shadowing of claims adjusters to map the end-to-end claims lifecycle, identifying manual bottlenecks and opportunities for data-driven intervention.
  • Tech Stack Evaluation: Perform a technical deep-dive to assess the current state and maturity of Databricks, Power BI, and Palantir, identifying data gaps and integration needs.

Phase II (Days 31-60): Strategy & Quick-Wins

In this phase, we translate discovery into a concrete plan. We will synthesize our findings into a coherent strategy and, crucially, identify a high-impact, low-effort "quick-win" project to demonstrate immediate value and build organizational momentum for the broader transformation.

  • KPI Definition Workshop: Facilitate a cross-functional workshop to gain consensus on a core set of measurable business outcomes that will define success for the program.
  • Use Case Prioritization: Host an ideation session to brainstorm and score potential use cases on an impact/effort matrix, selecting the best candidate for the quick-win project.
  • Develop a Prototype: Create a tangible, interactive mockup of the quick-win project (e.g., a Power BI dashboard) to gather early user feedback and refine requirements before full development.

Phase III (Days 61-100): Execution & Value Demonstration

This is where strategy becomes reality. The focus is on flawlessly delivering the quick-win project, quantifying its business impact, and using this success as a powerful proof point to gain executive buy-in for the long-term strategic roadmap.

  • Deliver Quick-Win Project: Execute the end-to-end development and deployment of the prioritized project, such as the "Cedent Data Quality Dashboard," ensuring a seamless user experience.
  • Value Socialization: Craft a compelling, data-driven story to showcase the business outcomes and value delivered by the quick-win, communicating this through demos and presentations.
  • Roadmap Presentation: Present the full, multi-year strategic plan to the senior leadership team, using the quick-win's success to secure the sponsorship and resources needed for the long-term vision.

Multi-Year Transformation Roadmap

A "crawl, walk, run" approach to systematically build a mature, data-driven claims organization, delivering continuous, incremental value.

Centralized Claims Data Model

The absolute foundation. We will implement the full Medallion Architecture in Databricks, unifying disparate data from all business lines into a single, governed source of truth for all downstream analytics.

Operational BI Suite

Democratize data by rolling out a suite of standardized Power BI dashboards, providing real-time visibility into operations and replacing thousands of hours of manual reporting.

Claims Leakage Reduction

Launch a high-ROI analytics program to systematically identify and reduce overpayments and missed subrogation opportunities, delivering a direct and measurable impact on the bottom line.

Initial Predictive Models

Move from hindsight to foresight by deploying our first ML models for Claim Severity Triage and Subrogation Potential Identification, enabling proactive claims management.

Advanced Fraud Detection

Evolve from simple rule-based systems to sophisticated, ML-based fraud detection in Databricks, tightly integrated with Palantir for uncovering complex, organized fraud rings.

Litigation Propensity Models

Proactively manage legal costs by developing models that forecast the probability of litigation, enabling early intervention and more effective settlement strategies.

External Data Integration

Enrich our analytical capabilities by systematically integrating high-value external data sources, such as geospatial imagery, real-time weather feeds, and economic indicators.

Pilot Prescriptive Analytics

Begin piloting "next-best-action" models that provide real-time recommendations to adjusters on optimal settlement offers, repair strategies, and expert assignments.

Operationalize Generative AI

Revolutionize adjuster efficiency by implementing LLMs to automate the summarization of unstructured claim documents, legal reports, and medical records, freeing thousands of hours for higher-value work.

Embed Prescriptive Analytics

Fully operationalize and embed AI-driven recommendations for triage, reserving, settlement, and recovery directly into the core claims management workflow, making intelligence actionable.

Claims as an Enterprise Risk Signal

Achieve the ultimate vision: establishing the claims data hub as a primary source of real-time, granular risk intelligence that dynamically informs underwriting decisions and pricing models.

"Touchless" Claims Processing

Achieve full straight-through processing (STP) for simple, low-risk claims using a combination of AI and process automation, delivering unparalleled efficiency and customer satisfaction.

The End-to-End Data Flow

Follow a claim's journey from a raw cedent file to an actionable insight, showcasing the seamless integration of our technology stack.

1

Ingestion

Cedent submits PDF loss notice and Excel bordereau to cloud storage.

2

ETL & Cleansing (Databricks)

Auto Loader ingests files. Delta Live Tables pipeline uses OCR to parse the PDF, cleanses the data, and loads it into conformed Silver layer tables.

3

AI/ML Scoring (Databricks)

A pre-trained ML model scores the new claim in real-time, flagging it with a high probability of litigation. Aggregated Gold tables are updated.

4

BI Alert & Visualization (Power BI)

The Claims Manager's dashboard refreshes, flashing a "High Litigation Risk" alert. The manager drills through to a detailed report.

5

Deep Investigation (Palantir)

The high-risk claim is pushed to Palantir. An investigator uses graph analytics to uncover a network of related suspicious claims from the same attorney, enabling a proactive legal strategy.
