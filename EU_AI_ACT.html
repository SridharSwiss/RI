The World's First
AI Constitution.

Navigating the European Union's landmark regulation. A risk-based framework designed to foster innovation while safeguarding fundamental human rights.

01. Scope

Extra-territorial reach. Applies to anyone putting AI into the EU market, regardless of where the provider is located.

02. Enforcement

Fines up to €35M or 7% of global turnover. Managed by the newly established European AI Office.

03. GPAI Models

Specific rules for General Purpose AI (ChatGPT, Claude). Systemic risk models face tier-two obligations.
